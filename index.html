Courses We Offer

EHJMC offers a variety of acdemic services

Junior Secondary School

EHJMC offers Junior Secondary School education from entry level (JS1) to exam level (JS3). The students will be taught a variety of subjects that will provide them with basic knowledge required to proceed in whatever discipline they choose.

Senior Secondary School

EHJMC offers Senior Secondary School education from entry level (SS1) to exam level (SS3). Unlike in junior class, the students will be split into one of Science, Arts and Commercial classes based on the discipline they wish to choose in their various universities.

Extension Classes

EHJMC has been know to produce one of the best external exam results not by malpractice but by taking it upon themselves to hold extension classes to adequately prepare their students for the exams. The classes are from JS3 to SS3 and it is a method that has worked for years.
