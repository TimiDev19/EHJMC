Brief History

Eucharistic Heart Of Jesus Model College, Asa-Dam Road, Ilorin, Kwara State was established in 1999 by the Sisters of the Eucharistic Heart of Jesus Congregation. It is a co-educational with a view to fostering and inculcating good and lasting moral values to young ones.
Among other things, the Sisters who founded this College have a sole aim of instilling genuine discipline and moral uprightness into our educational system in particular and into the polity in general, with a view to turning out leaders who will sanitise our pluralistic Nigeria society and the world at large. Effort is in top gear vigorously to achieve its desired success.
The foundation of the school was laid by the Catholic Bishop of Ilorin, His Lordship, Most Rev Dr. Ayo-Maria Atoyebi (OP) on 17th May 1999 and the take-off was on 27th september 1999 with eleven students. To the greater glory of God, the track performance record of the college at WAEC and NECO has been 100% since inception.

Our Philosophy

Eucharistic Heart Of Jesus Model College is under the patronage of the Eucharistic Heart of Jesus. We draw Our Inspiration from the Love that Jesus shared with us in His Most Holy Eucharist.

Our Mission

To give qualitative and all-embracing education to young boys and girls so as to develop a service of purpose and awareness of their roles as members of the society.

Our Vision

To foster young adults into competent leaders who will be sound in character, morals and academic standing.
